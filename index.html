Welcome to Auto Write

Auto Write is a Chrome extension that writes inputted text to a Google Doc open in your browser. The purpose is to allow people to watch text written live to a Google document as it is known to be beneficial for studying.

New research has found that watching text develop as it's written on a page or screen increases our ability to remember said text. Auto Write's purpose is to use JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Google Docs APIs to provide users with this increased productivity study habit.
