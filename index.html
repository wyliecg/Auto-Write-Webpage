What is Auto Write?

Auto Write is a Chrome extension designed to boost your study efficiency by allowing you to watch text being written in real-time in Google Docs. This innovative approach leverages the power of dynamic writing to help you retain information better.

By integrating seamlessly with Google Docs, Auto Write provides an engaging way to take notes and review material, making your study sessions more productive and effective.
